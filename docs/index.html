11 optical stages.
Zero compromise.

Every processing stage runs in scene-linear light space, preserving your highlights and dynamic range. GPU-accelerated on Metal, with CPU fallback.

Depth-of-Field Bokeh

Physically-based thin-lens model. Golden-angle spiral sampling, adjustable blade count, curvature, cat-eye vignetting, and swirl.

Chromatic Aberration

Lateral radial RGB displacement calibrated per lens. Accurate fringing that scales with resolution.

Vignette & Color Cast

Exposure falloff in stops with natural power curve. Per-lens edge color shift in CIE xy chromaticity.

Distortion Warp

Brown–Conrady model with k1/k2/k3 radial and p1/p2 tangential coefficients. Optional scale-to-frame.

Bloom & Veiling Glare

Multi-scale thresholded blur with per-lens tint. Adds that cinematic halation around highlights.

Anamorphic Mode

Squeeze factors (1.25x–2x), oval bokeh, horizontal streak flares, blue flare bias. 10 anamorphic presets included.

Coma

Directional anisotropic blur radiating from center. Per-lens hue bias gives characteristic sagittal/tangential smearing.

f-Stop Sharpener

MTF microcontrast restoration with highlight protection and wide-open glow awareness. Not a generic sharpen.

50 cinema lenses.
Mix and match traits.

From modern clinical primes to vintage character glass. Every preset has calibrated distortion, bokeh, CA, vignette, bloom, fringing, and sharpness.

Mix & Match System

Override any trait group with a different lens. Want Master Prime sharpness with Cooke Panchro bokeh and Helios swirl? Done.
